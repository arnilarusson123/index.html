About

Short bio

I’m an Icelandic actor and voice actor based in Reykjavík, working under the name Árni Þór Lárusson (Arni Larusson). I trained at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA) and work across Icelandic and international productions.

Recent screen work includes The Darkness, True Detective: Night Country and the Netflix feature Against the Ice. On stage I’ve appeared in productions such as Macbeth, 9 Líf, Fyrrverandi, Pinocchio and Emil of Lönneberga at Reykjavík City Theatre.

In voice and games I’ve played Peter Parker / Spider-Man in the Icelandic dub of Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse, Sichfrith in Assassin’s Creed Valhalla – Wrath of the Druids and the Lord of Khorne in Total War: Warhammer III.
