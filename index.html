Profile

About & selected credits

I’m an Icelandic actor and voice actor based in Reykjavík. I trained at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA) and work across Icelandic and international productions on screen, stage and in video games.

Recent screen work includes The Darkness, True Detective: Night Country and Against the Ice. On stage I’ve appeared in productions such as Macbeth, 9 Líf, Fyrrverandi, Pinocchio and Emil of Lönneberga at Reykjavík City Theatre.

In voice and games my work includes Peter Parker / Spider-Man in the Icelandic dub of Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse, Sichfrith in Assassin’s Creed Valhalla – Wrath of the Druids and the Lord of Khorne in Total War: Warhammer III.
