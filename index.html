About

Profile

I’m Árni Þór Lárusson — an Icelandic actor and voice actor based in Reykjavík. I trained at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA) and work across stage, film, television and video games in both Icelandic and English.

My recent screen work includes The Darkness (2024), True Detective: Night Country (2023) and Against the Ice (2021). I’ve performed in major productions at Reykjavík City Theatre, including Macbeth, the musical 9 Líf, Fyrrverandi, Pinocchio, Emil of Lönneberga and Veisla.

In voice acting, I’ve played Spider-Man in the Icelandic dub of Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse, Sichfrith in Assassin’s Creed Valhalla – Wrath of the Druids, and the Lord of Khorne in Total War: Warhammer III.

I’m drawn to character-driven stories and love collaborating with filmmakers, theatres and game studios. I’m open to new projects in Iceland and internationally.
