About

Profile

I’m an Icelandic actor and voice actor based in Reykjavík, working under the name Árni Þór Lárusson (Arni Larusson).

I trained at the London Academy of Music and Dramatic Art (LAMDA) and work across Icelandic and international stage, film, television and video games. My recent screen work includes The Darkness (2024), True Detective: Night Country (2023) and the Netflix feature Against the Ice (2021).

At Reykjavík City Theatre (Borgarleikhúsið) I’ve appeared in productions such as Macbeth (as Malcolm), the musical 9 Líf (as Bubbi Morthens), Fyrrverandi (as Bjartur), Pinocchio (title role), Emil of Lönneberga (as Erik the Auctioneer) and Veisla (as Berndsen).

In voice acting, I’ve played Peter Parker / Spider-Man in the Icelandic dub of Spider-Man: Across the Spider-Verse, Sichfrith in Assassin’s Creed Valhalla – Wrath of the Druids and the Lord of Khorne in Total War: Warhammer III.

I’m drawn to character-driven stories and enjoy collaborating with filmmakers, theatres and game studios. I’m open to new projects in Iceland and internationally.
